about our school

Holberton School is a Computer Science school founded in Silicon Valley to address the gap in the education system for aspiring software engineers. After Holberton School opened its doors in 2016, the world’s most innovative companies have noticed. Graduates have found jobs at LinkedIn, Google, Tesla, Docker, Apple, Dropbox, Facebook, Pinterest, Genentech, Cisco, IBM, and more.

Welcome to Baku, Azerbaijan

about our campus

Baku, the vibrant heart of the Caucasus, offers a captivating blend of tradition and innovation. As the city stretches along the Caspian Sea coast, it showcases a mesmerizing mix of historic landmarks and modern skyscrapers. This city is more than just an architectural marvel; it is rapidly emerging as a dynamic hub for technology in the wider Central Asian region.

Conveniently located in the heart of Baku, the campus serves as an epicenter of innovation and learning. Easily accessible from all corners of Baku, this hub is more than just its strategic location. It is a beacon for aspiring tech professionals, offering cutting-edge facilities and a curriculum tailored to meet the evolving needs of tech industry. With the city's lively backdrop and the commitment to excellence, Baku is undoubtedly positioning itself as a pivotal destination for technology enthusiasts and professionals alike.
